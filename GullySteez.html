Mission Statement

Gully Steez runs a digital media network comprised of multiple brands in different niches. We carefully curate content for our brands and host creative projects with two main goals.

  1. Nurture creativity through our monthly art contests
  2. Provide people with entertaining content after a long day at work or school

Sketchathon Art Club

You don't need a flashy portfolio or a long resumé to join in on this art contest. All drawings and paintings are accepted and all experience levels will have a chance to win cash prizes.

Steez Wars Blackbook Graffiti Battles

Steez Wars is a monthly blackbook graffiti battle with cash prizes. Sometimes we run strictly hand style battles. Sometimes we do character battles. And sometimes it's all about the wild style. You never really know what we got going on but we ALWAYS have something going on. Give it a shot!

Sketchaholics Themed Art Contests

Are you an artist that enjoys a challenge? Sketchaholics runs themed art contests with cash prizes. We're huge cartoon fans so most of our themes revisit our childhoods but we also value community opinions and run polls to draft our schedules. Check the contest site to see what we currently have running!

Secret Poetry Stash

Are you a poet looking to make a positive impact with your work? Secret Poetry Stash collects poetry to create care packages for people in need. We've given away hundreds of pieces to dozens of individuals and have had participants from over 12 different countries. Join our family today!
